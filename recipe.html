Mac And Cheese

    Ingrediends

  • butter
  • milk
  • macaroni
  • cheese

    How to Make:

  1. Put the macaroni in a pot full of water and boil it for 6-7 mins or until soft
  2. Strain the water of the macaroni and keep it aside
  3. In a bowl mix butter and milk in 2:6 ratio
  4. Add the macaroni to the bowl and mix until evenly

SERVE AND ENJOY WHILE HOT
